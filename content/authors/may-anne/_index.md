bio: "Dr. May Anne E. Mata is a full professor of applied mathematics in the Department of Mathematics, Physics, and Computer Science at the University of the Philippines Mindanao and a fellow of the UP Resilience Institute. She finished her bachelor's study in BS Applied Mathematics at UP Mindanao (cum laude and class valedictorian) and her master's degree in Applied Mathematics at the University of Washington. In 2017, she earned her PhD in Interdisciplinary Studies specializing in Mathematical Biology from the University of British Columbia. Dr. Mata was awarded the 2021 Gawad Chanselor sa Pinakamahusay na Guro (Senior category) and a UP Scientist in the same institution. Her expertise is in operations research, disease modeling, differential equations, and multivariate data analysis.
